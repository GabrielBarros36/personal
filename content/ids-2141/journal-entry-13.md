My game is called "America". Its signature feature is that the player spawns at the center of two rows of fighter jets taking off of a ramp while fireworks go off.

Making the game was pretty simple. For the "floor" I just stretched a standard cube object to make it large enough. The ramp is another cube stretched and rotated. Both the floor and ramp have textures from the standard asset pack. The planes themselves were also from the Standard Asset Pack, and fortunately for me their behavior of taking off as soon as the game starts is the default. The only package I used was the [Unity Standard Assets Pack](https://github.com/jamschutz/Unity-Standard-Assets/) shown to us in class.
