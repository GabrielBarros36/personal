My creation is a headphone holder for my desk. I wear my headphones a lot, but when I'm not they're always taking up space on my desk. With this headphone holder I get to keep my headphones close without them taking up valuable real estate on my desk.

This was very straighforward to make. I designed the object in Autodesk Fusion, exported it as a STL file, and uploaded it to the 3D printer portal the Innovation Hub uses. The print worked out on the first try.
