My idea is to make a headphone holder that hooks under my desk. My headphones are always lying flat on my desk, taking up space. I could leave them inside their case but that doesn't allow me to charge them. The perfect solution is to stick them where they won't take valuable space and where they're readily reachable. The space under my desk fulfills both requirements.
