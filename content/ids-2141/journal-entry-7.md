## Cable Holder
This cable holder hooks onto the back of my desk. The top part extends forward so that the object's weight is shifted far forward. It has little notches at the front where you can hang the cables while they're not in use-  that way they don't fall behind the desk.

Making this was fairly simple - once I had an idea of the object I wanted to make, I started with one big solid cube and started poking holes into it. FreeCAD's filleting feature was easy to use for that too.
